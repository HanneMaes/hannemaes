12 Years

A unique artwork, every hour, for 12 years.

3 structures, each rotating at a different speed.
The left one rotates in a 7 days, the middle one in 30 days and the right one takes 365 days complete it's cycle
It takes 12 years for the whole work to complete all the different configurations and arrive back at it's origin state.
